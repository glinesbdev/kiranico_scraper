Specialized Tools
Vitality Mantle Negates a fixed amount of damage, after which the effect expires.
Ghillie Mantle Conceals you from enemy view temporarily. The effect expires if you perform an attack or take damage.
Glider Mantle Increases jump airtime and grants the power to ride strong winds. Also makes it easier to mount monsters.
Challenger Mantle Prompts monsters to target you more often and makes them easier to lure. The effect expires if you are attacked.
Bandit Mantle Causes monsters to drop rare trade-in items when you attack them.
Apothecary Mantle Increases the probability of triggering status buildup when attacking with weapons that induce abnormal statuses.
Immunity Mantle Removes and nullifies all abnormal status effects for the duration of the effect.
Impact Mantle Adds a stun effect to most attacks or increases the potency of an existing stun effect.
Evasion Mantle Increases the invulnerability window while evading and grants a temporary attack boost for evading a monster attack at the very last moment.
Rocksteady Mantle Eliminates damage reactions, prevents wind effects, protects your hearing, and grants tremor resistance. You also receive less damage from attacks.
Fireproof Mantle Reduces fire damage, nullifies fireblight and blastblight, and prevents damage from hot environments.
Waterproof Mantle Reduces water damage, nullifies waterblight, reduces mobility restrictions in muck and negates them entirely for deep water.
Iceproof Mantle Reduces ice damage and nullifies iceblight.
Thunderproof Mantle Reduces thunder damage and nullifies thunderblight and paralysis.
Dragonproof Mantle Reduces dragon damage, nullifies dragonblight, and powers up your weapon's dragon properties.
Temporal Mantle Nullifies damage from powerful attacks by automatically evading.
Assassin's Hood Increases movement speed and allows you to hide quicker. Deals big damage to a monster caught unaware, then the effect ends.
Health Booster Places a smoker that gradually restores your health.
Cleanser Booster Places a smoker that removes any abnormal status effects and elemental blights.
Affinity Booster Places a smoker that increases affinity.
